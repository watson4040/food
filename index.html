About Rashmy's Food

We specialize in shawarma, fresh cakes, and soft chapati – made with love daily. Our shawarma is marinated for hours, cakes are baked fresh, and chapati is rolled by hand. Whether you crave a quick wrap or a sweet treat, we deliver across Lusaka.

We offer takeaway and free delivery within Lusaka for orders above K150. Our mission is to make every meal feel like home.
